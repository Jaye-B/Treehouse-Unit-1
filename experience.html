Professional Experience

Teaching in New Jersey

My journey with education started while I was in college. While in college I started working with underserved children growing up in communities that put them at an automatic disadvantage in life.

I began volunteering my time outside of school hours coaching and getting involved in the community with varying projects. This eventually led to working in after school programs for a few years.

Once I graduated college I eventually went on to become a middle school English Language Arts, Special Education, and English as a Second Language Teacher.

Teaching in China

I've always known I wanted to travel, live, and work abroad. That desire was increased once I learned about the need for english language teachers in many countries abroad.

I jumped at the opportunity to teach english to middle school students in China. Although my experience was quite affected by the COVID-19 pandemic, it was still an incredible opportunity I am wholeheartedly grateful for.

Here is a great resource if you're interested in looking into english teaching jobs outside of the United States: Dave's ESL Cafe
